How Xandeum pNode rewards work

How are rewards calculated?

Each pNode earns a share of the per-Yuga STOINC pool based on its contribution weight = storage × performance score × active stake × boost factor. Your share is your weight divided by the total network weight. 90% of the wSOL pool each Yuga goes to operators, 5% to Xandeum Platform Corporation and 5% to the Foundation.

What is STOINC and a Yuga?

STOINC (Storage Income) turns real storage activity into wSOL fee pools for operators. It settles once per Yuga, roughly every 2.8 days. So a year holds about 130 Yugas.

What is the boost factor?

A multiplier on your weight. It combines your purchase-era boost with up to two NFT boosts that multiply together. A 1.2× and a 1.5× NFT give a 1.8× NFT boost; multiply that by your era boost for the total.

What's live vs a scenario?

The network total weight is live now: we sum storage × performance × stake across every node from self-sourced per-node data (the same feed that powers the dashboard's Storage column). The only remaining scenario is the wSOL pool per Yuga, which isn't known until a Yuga closes. Per-node boost factors aren't public, so the live total is boost-agnostic; your own boost still multiplies your share.
